Chicken Noodle




Description



A healthy, satisfying soup.

Ingredients

  • 2 T butter
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 large carrots, chopped
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 3 sprigs thyme
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless, chicken thighs
  • 8 cups chicken stock
  • 5 oz egg noodles
  • salt and pepper, to taste
